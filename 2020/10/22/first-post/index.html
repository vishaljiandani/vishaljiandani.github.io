Hello Blogging World

October 22, 2020


I never thought I’d get this far.

This is surreal. I’ve just made my first website. I’ve never been more proud of myself than I am right now.

A lot of my friends, both here and back home, suggested I make a portfolio website to display all my work and “stand out” to recruiters. While that was part of the reason for starting this, I’ve always wanted to get into blogging and rediscover my passion for writing. It’s something that I used to be quite good at as a kid, but as with so many things, it just faded. Well, here’s to reviving an old habit that died hard.

As of writing this post, the only things you, the reader, are able to see right now is my projects and said blog. I do plan on adding more things as I go along, but this is good for now.

Enjoy! And for the love of God, wear a mask!
